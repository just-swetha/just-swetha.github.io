Approach

Research
first.
Always.

Every project here began with listening: interviews, observation, and careful attention to what people actually need rather than what they say they want. The work spans mobile app UI, experience design, and interactive systems.

Swetha Siva holds an MFA in Creative Writing from the University of Miami and is completing an MA in Emerging Media Design and Development at Ball State University. Her background in narrative and qualitative research shapes how she approaches design: understanding the person first, the system second.
